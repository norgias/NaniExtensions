Freequently Asked Questions



• How is your Virgin Hair different?

A: Our virgin hair has not been touched or tampered with by chemical processes such chemicals, perms, relaxers and dyes.



• How long will the hair last?

A: 6-12 months



How much does your hair weigh?

A: Each of our bundles are approximately 3.5-4.0 ounces



• How much hair do I need?

A: The average person needs 8-10 ounces for a full install.



We Recomend


2 bundles for 12-16’ inches
3 bundles for 18-24’ inches
4 bundles for 26-30’ inches
