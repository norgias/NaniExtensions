Thank you for your interest in Nani Extensions Wholesale Program!



Starting your OWN business can be fun, rewarding, and give you the freedom in life you’ve always wanted! If you’re interested in becoming a distributor of Nani Extensions or would like to develop your own private brand we’d love to do business with YOU!

  Aspiring entrepreneurs, hair lovers, beauty bloggers, hair stylist or salons; we want to be able to provide you with competitive pricing and also the BEST quality hair. All wholesale orders require a minimum purchase of 10 pieces or more (bundles, closures, clip-ins, micro loops included in piece count) for EACH order.

 The wholesale discount is $20 off of the current retail price PER piece. All wholesale orders will be charged a 2% processing fee. Wholesale orders will be packed and shipped via USPS It’s just that easy!

To complete a wholesale order, contact us via email. For those wanting to sell products under your own company name, we are more than willing to assist you by providing extra packaging bags, labels, and selling tools
